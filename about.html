E-Gamers Café LLC is bringing a new perspective to social, competitive & casual gaming. Founded by Gamers, our goal is to provide a one of a kind gaming area for Gamers of all ages to come and socialize. We have 12 high end computers (1070 graphics cards, intel i7), 1 GB internet speeds (Fiber Optic), 3 stations with consoles, one of a kind ambiance, custom equipment, and a unique café with a Cuban flare. The 12 computers are elevated on a stage, where 2 teams of up to 6 players compete against one another! These tournaments will also be streamed on a massive projection screen, so everyone will see your skills! So it's time to get out of the dark and into the light, at E-Gamers Cafe!
